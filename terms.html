Section 02

Services Provided

BookdAI provides a done-for-you appointment setting system for health and life insurance agents. Services included in your plan are:

  • Lead Data — Fresh, digitally opted-in lead records sourced and delivered weekly based on your selected plan. No extra charge.
  • AI Texting Pipeline — AI-powered SMS outreach conducted via your dedicated phone number.
  • Ghost Re-engage — Multi-touch automated follow-up sequences for non-responding leads.
  • Drip Sequences — Long-term automated nurture campaigns for aged and stale leads.
  • AI Qualification — AI bot qualifies prospects prior to booking to ensure appointment quality.
  • Direct Calendar Booking — Confirmed appointments booked directly into your connected calendar.
  • Hot Lead List — Daily list of warm, opted-in leads generated for your direct outreach.
  • Lead Re-engagement Queue — Opted-in leads are automatically cycled back through re-engagement sequences.
  • GHL Setup & Management — Full system setup and ongoing management inside your GoHighLevel (GHL) sub-account.
  • Texting Costs Included — All SMS messaging costs are included in your weekly plan fee. No separate texting charges apply.
