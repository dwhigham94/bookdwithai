What BookdAI does

BookdAI is operated by Invictus Marketing Group LLC, a Florida limited liability company. We provide appointment scheduling, reminder, and client communication services using SMS text messaging — only with people who have given their prior express consent to be contacted.

Appointment scheduling

When a client or prospect requests information or a consultation, BookdAI coordinates a time by text and places it directly on the calendar — no phone tag.

Reminders & confirmations

Automated confirmation and reminder messages reduce no-shows and keep appointments on track, with easy rescheduling by reply.

Service updates

Opted-in contacts receive relevant account and service notifications — and can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.
